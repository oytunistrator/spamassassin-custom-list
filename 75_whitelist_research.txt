## Rationale
These domains are either regulated financial institutions, government bodies,
or large services with strong SPF/DKIM/DMARC policies.  They are frequent
phishing targets, so whitelisting their legitimate mail reduces false
positives while the ruleset still catches forged phishing attempts through
SPF/DKIM/DMARC failures.
